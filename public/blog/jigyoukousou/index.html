About My Master Thesis: Business Concept (事業構想)

March 13, 2018    Business Project Africa Investment

My Master thesis work has been fully supported by the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) as part of African Business Education (ABE) initiative 2nd batch.

Many people are willing to invest in Africa, but they don’t have enough information about what’s going on in the continent. This may be forth one of the reasons for their reluctance. I talked to several Japanese companies during some business networking programs (organized by JICA) to propose some business ideas for Africa, I realized that few of them are ready to invest in Africa in the near future while others do not know where to start.–They fear about high risk due to the business climate.–They highly rely on mouth-to-ear information related to some unsuccessful experiences in Africa.–They trust Doing Business platform more than the African Development Bank (AfDB) data.

  • With 54 countries and population estimated at 1.2 billion (Worldometers, 2018);

  • With a considerable rise of education rate (AfDB, 2017).

Africa has become a target for many companies, organizations and countries in terms of businesses.

Doing Business is a web application of the World Bank that provides the measures of business regulations, and makes a benchmark by countries in a clear and compelling way to facilitate the choice of good countries. Otherwise the AfDB manages projects that engage the future of business in Africa. Unfortunately, its project portfolio does not provide attractive insights to intended audiences.

In this thesis, we built machine learning models to transform project data into actionable insights for helping investors to seize opportunities that the African market offers.
