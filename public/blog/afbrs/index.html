The Borderless Education Organization

Africa Bridge and Research Society

Posted July 18, 2018, slightly revised July 30, 2018    Research Ubuntu Development Education

We are very excited to announce the creation of the Africa Bridge and Research Society (AfBRS). Our organization aims to function as a bridge between researchers across the continent. Our vision is to introduce the rules of borderless education in Africa by supporting promising research topics (from data collection to publication) and creating a starting point for discussion on what research seeds Africa should invest in.


We AfBRS strongly believe that research is the electricity that will trigger the development of the African continent and education is the wire to achieve this.


Let’s transform Africa with research!


For all inquiries, contact us at contact@afbrs.org.
